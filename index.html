Personal Life

Scott Thompson, Carrot Top, grew up in Cocoa, FL and graduated from Cocoa High in 1983. His nickname came from a local swimming coach due to his red hair. He's been an active comedian since 1989 and currently has a contract where he performs at the Luxor in Las Vegas, NV.
